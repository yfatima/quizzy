Help and Documentation

Quizzie is an application that offers its users a variety of quizzes that determine important things such as: selecting a favorite coffee flavor, or deciding your major. Each quiz in quizzie consists of 5 questions with 4 answers, and you must select an answer before you move on to the next question.

General Quiz

The general quiz is designed to ask you questions which will assist in determining choices available to you. Each quiz consists of 5 questions with 4 answers. You are prompted with a question and cannot proceed unless an answer is given.

Emoji Quiz

The emoji quiz consists of 4 questions involving images which hints towards an answer.
